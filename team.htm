Sunil Kumar Ganeriwal

Mr. Sunil Kumar Ganeriwal, B. Com. (Hons.), FCA, CMA, ACS, ISA (ICAI) is the founder Partner of the Firm having more than 23 years of Professional experience having independently handled all kinds of audits i.e. Statutory Audit, Tax Audit, Concurrent Audit, Liquidators assignments, Bank audits, Company Law & Income Tax Matters, Project Consultancy etc. He has secured rank in the Finals of the CA and ICWA exams on all over India basis and in the Intermediate examination of CS. He is well versed with all major accounting and auditing software and has worked in different EDP environments in his career. He has also completed Information System audit course conducted by ICAI in the first attempt.
